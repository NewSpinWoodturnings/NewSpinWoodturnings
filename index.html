How It's Made

From log to heirloom

I

Select the Wood

Every piece starts with choosing a blank that has something to say — interesting grain, figure, or a story in its rings.

II

Turn on the Lathe

The blank is mounted and shaped at speed. This is where the form emerges — walls thinned, curves refined, the profile set.

III

Hand Sand & Refine

Sanded through progressive grits by hand, bringing out the wood's natural lustre and ensuring the surface is perfectly smooth.

IV

Oil & Finish

Finished with food-safe oils that penetrate the grain, protecting the wood while letting its natural beauty speak for itself.
